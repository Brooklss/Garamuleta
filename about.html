Our Story

Two Decades of Excellence

Founded in 2005, GARA Construction Solutions PLC has grown from a small import business to become one of Ethiopia's most trusted suppliers of premium construction finishing materials. Our journey has been marked by an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

We specialize in importing and distributing high-quality, contemporary construction finishing materials including flooring, wall cladding, ceiling systems, sanitary fixtures, paints, and decorative products. Our extensive network of international suppliers ensures that we bring only the best materials to the Ethiopian market.

Over the years, we have had the privilege of working on hundreds of residential and commercial projects, from luxury hotels to modern office complexes, earning a reputation for reliability, expertise, and exceptional service.

Our Mission

To provide the Ethiopian construction industry with access to world-class finishing materials, backed by expert consultation and reliable service, enabling our clients to create exceptional spaces that stand the test of time.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative construction finishing solutions in Ethiopia, recognized for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer excellence, while contributing to the country's modern infrastructure development.
