Hospitality

Luxury Hotel Addis Ababa

Bole, Addis Ababa
Completed: 2024
12,000 sq.m

Project Overview

A premium 5-star hotel featuring 150 luxury rooms and suites. GARA provided complete interior finishing materials including Italian marble flooring in the lobby, premium ceramic tiles in guest bathrooms, and elegant wall cladding throughout public spaces.

Materials Used

  • Italian Marble Flooring - Lobby & Corridors
  • Premium Ceramic Tiles - 150 Bathrooms
  • Designer Wall Tiles - Public Areas
  • Luxury Sanitary Fixtures - All Bathrooms
  • Suspended Ceiling Systems - Conference Rooms
  • Premium Paint Finishes - All Interior Walls
